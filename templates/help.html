How does this work?

For each act / kink / fantasy…

… click "G" if you've "given" that actvity, "R" if you've "received" it, (turning it red means you've done it; click again to turn it back to grey if you made a mistake),

… click the grey boxes to choose your interest in doing it in future,

and you can use the notes area for anything which doesn't fit elsewhere.

Given / received?

For BDSM, "given" means "top", & "received" means bottom – or closest role, e.g. for age play "given" could mean "older".

"Worship" / "service" is similar, "received" means "made to do it", "given" means "had someone do it".

With things like "leather clothing", "given" means seeing, "received" means wearing.

Saving & sharing

When you're done, click ⚙ › "Save quiz to file", then send it (e.g. via Signal message).
