How does this work?

For each act / kink / fantasy…

… click "G" if you've "given" that actvity, "R" if you've "received" it,

… click the grey boxes to choose your interest in doing it in future,

and you can use the notes area for anything which doesn't fit elsewhere.

Given / received?

For BDSM, "given" means "top", & "received" means bottom

(or closest role, e.g. for age play "given" could mean "older")

For "worship" / "service", "received" means did it, "given" means ordered someone to.

Saving & sharing

When you're done, click ⚙ › "Save quiz to file", then send it (e.g. via e-mail).
